# The Oidhán Library

**Last updated: June 13, 2026 | 75 published papers**

## Buy Me Time. I'll Build the Rest.

I'm Eric Don McElroy, an inventor in West Knoxville, Tennessee. Over the past few months I've written more than 140 technical papers — energy, agriculture, manufacturing, computing, water, shelter, resource recovery. The common thread: everything is closed-loop, non-toxic, and designed to leave things better than it found them.

I don't know if all of it works. Some of it might fail. Some might only work at small scale. Some might need someone smarter than me to fix my mistakes. But I want to find out — and I can't do that alone.

I'm broke. Most of my time goes to just surviving. The papers are free. The IP is open. What I need is time — to build prototypes, run tests, and work with the people who can tell me where I'm wrong.

That's the whole ask. Not "believe in me." Not "fund my genius." Just: buy me time. If I have time, I can build. If I build, we can test. If we test, we learn.

A few dollars a month covers rent and keeps me designing. Enough people, and I can afford an assistant for the formatting and outreach I'm bad at. One person with deeper pockets who decides this is worth a serious look, and the whole thing accelerates.

**What you get:** a public log of everything I build and test; your name in the acknowledgments of every paper, if you want it; and a standing invitation to tell me where I'm wrong. I mean that last one.

**What you don't get:** paywalls — everything stays free. And no polished startup pitch. I'm not a company. I'm a guy with a library of ideas and a soldering iron.

**Support this work:** [https://ko-fi.com/oidhan](https://ko-fi.com/oidhan)
